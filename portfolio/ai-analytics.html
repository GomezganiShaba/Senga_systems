Machine learning-powered business intelligence platform for real-time analytics and data-driven decision making.

Client Enterprise Corporation
Duration 14 Weeks
Services AI/ML, Data Analytics, Development

Project Overview

An enterprise corporation struggling with fragmented data sources and slow reporting processes approached Senga Systems to build a unified analytics platform powered by artificial intelligence.

Our data science team developed a comprehensive business intelligence dashboard that leverages machine learning to provide predictive insights, anomaly detection, and automated reporting in real-time.

The Challenge

The client needed to:

  • Enable real-time analytics for faster decision making
  • Consolidate data from multiple disparate sources
  • Implement predictive models for business forecasting
  • Automate report generation and anomaly alerts
  • Provide intuitive visualizations for non-technical users
